Event Horizon is a concept album centered around the emotional turmoil brought about by the COVID-19 pandemic. We sought to take listeners through the wide variety of emotions that we felt during a time of great loss, uncertainty, and isolation.

As the album's recording engineer and producer, I had the great privilege of working with an unparalleled group of arrangers, vocalists, and engineers. The hundreds of hours spent recording in the studio and overseeing the album's creative direction outside of the studio were some of my best memories during my college experience.

I also performed on this album, largely as a vocal percussionist (_When Demons Come to Life_, _Drink to Drown_, _Welcome to the Black Parade_, _Unself Portrait_, _It's Called: Freefall_) and backing vocalist (almost all tracks). I also co-arranged and co-soloed on _Nightmare_.

[Full liner notes are available here](https://www.stanfordharmonics.com/event-horizon).
